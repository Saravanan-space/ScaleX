What is scaleX?

scaleX is a premier idea pitching event organized by the prestigious ICC club NEXGEN at the K.S. Institute of Technology. It is designed to be the launching pad for the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Whether you have a groundbreaking tech startup idea, a social initiative, or a unique product concept, scaleX provides you with the ultimate platform. Pitch your ideas in front of industry experts, gain immense exposure, and get the recognition you deserve to turn your vision into reality.

Rules & Regulations

Please read the following guidelines carefully before registering your pitch for the scaleX event.

Team Size

Participants can pitch individually or in a team of up to 4 members. Only one registration is required per team.

Time Limit

Each team will be given exactly 5 minutes for their pitch, followed by a strict 3-minute Q&A session with the judges.

Originality

All ideas must be 100% original. Plagiarized ideas or previously funded startups will be immediately disqualified.

Presentation

Presentations (PPT/PDF) must be submitted at least 24 hours prior to the event. A standard format is highly recommended.
